  4. SlopeNoise

SlopeNoise is a unique primitive specifically for creating expansive sloping terrains. SlopeNoise's internal noise distortions create a shape that is very conducive to strong, flowing erosion.

SlopeNoise is fantastic for medium range or close-up scenes, where you want to dramatically show the scale and height of the slope.


Node Properties
SettingDescription
ScaleThe scale of shapes on the slope.
DisplacementThe amount of displacement to perform on the slope shapes.
DirectionThe direction of the slope gradient.
StratifiedWhen enabled, creates long, stratified protrusions on the slope.
SeedThe randomization pattern or seed for the node's process.
