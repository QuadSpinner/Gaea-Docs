Breaker

Breaker creates large cracks in the terrain, following the general rules of Hydraulic Erosion, but without creating soil deposits.

Properties

Breaker

DurationThe number of breakage iterations to perform on the terrain. The default value should work in most situations.

River LengthThe maximum length of each break or river.

Erosion PowerThe strength of the breakage process. High values may cause sharp drops and spikes depending on the terrain.

DepthDepth of the cracks.

Advanced

SeedThe randomization pattern or seed for the node's process.

Hard CracksDisables antialiasing and smooth of the crack edges.

Mode

FastProcesses faster, but may not always result in the same pattern of cracks. AccurateProcesses a bit slower, but ensures the pattern remains the same.
